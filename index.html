MISSION:

Seeking to develop the balanced student-athlete with an emphasis on academic, athletic, and personal excellence.

As a team, we compete nationally in the American Collegiate Hockey Association (ACHA) Division 1 and as members of the Women's Midwest College Hockey League (WMCHL).

We emphasize the importance of academics, a balanced college life, and supporting our players as they prepare for their future endeavors! We do this by holding practices twice weekly on Tuesday and Thursday nights, for one hour. We have about 10 games per semester.
